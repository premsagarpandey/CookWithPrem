My Culinary Journey

Welcome to **CookWithPrem**! This project was started by **Prem Sagar Pandey** as a simple repository of recipe instructions. What began as a personal log of my favorite home-cooked meals quickly grew into a complete, beginner-focused guide to traditional cooking.

I believe that cooking is not an innate talent, but a skill that anyone can pick up with practice. The biggest obstacle for most beginners is when recipes omit simple steps or assume prior knowledge. That is why I design each recipe card to be completely self-explanatory, focusing on exact times, temperatures, and visuals.

My Cooking Principles

  • Keep it fresh: Use simple, fresh ingredients that are easily available in local grocery stores.
  • No shortcuts on prep: A clean and prepped workspace makes cooking enjoyable and stress-free.
  • Taste as you go: Adjust salt and spices at different stages of cooking.

Prem's Top 3 Tips for Beginners

1. Read the recipe fully first

Never start cooking mid-read. Read the entire recipe before chopping or turning on the stove, so you know what is coming next.

2. Prep everything in advance

Mise en place is key. Chop your vegetables and measure out spice mixes into small bowls before you heat any oil. It prevents burning ingredients while searching for spices.

3. Control your heat

High heat is not always faster. Most traditional curries and gravies release their best flavors when cooked slowly on low to medium heat.
