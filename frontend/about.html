My Culinary Journey

When I came to Bhopal for my B.Tech, I knew how to code... but I didn't know how to cook.

The first few weeks were not easy. I missed my home every single day, especially my mother's cooking. I tried eating outside, but I wasn't comfortable because many places didn't feel hygienic. I also joined a mess, but the food quality wasn't what I expected.

The biggest problem was that I didn't know how to cook anything.

Whenever I wanted to make a dish, I had to search Google again and again by typing the dish name. I couldn't find one simple place where all the recipes were organized together. It was time-consuming and frustrating.

That's when I got an idea.

Why not build a website where anyone can find recipes easily in one place?

That's how CookWithPrem was born. 💚

This website is for every student living away from home, every beginner who wants to learn cooking, and everyone who simply wants easy, tasty, and simple recipes without wasting time searching again and again.

I hope CookWithPrem makes your cooking journey a little easier—and maybe even brings you a little closer to the feeling of home.
